B'more on Rails is organizing a number of events for RailsConf 2010, which will be held in Baltimore June 7-10 2010.

Follow @bmoreonrails for the most up-to-date information!

Stay with a Local

As part of a larger effort to welcome the Rails community to Baltimore for RailsConf 2010, B'more on Rails has organized a room-share for RailsConf attendees. We have 12 private rooms available to attendees for the duration of the conference. We see it as an opportunity to welcome you to our city and for us to create a few new friendships.

BohConf

BohConf is the official RailsConf 2010 unconf. At BohConf, we're going to get our hands dirty writing code and sharing ideas in an open and free-form environment. This hacking-centric event will include community code drives featuring well known OSS authors, a code retreat, a programming competition, and barcamp-style discussions. It is free and will run alongside RailsConf in the convention center. Everyone is welcome at BohConf! Follow @bohconf. BohConf is organized by SmartLogic, a Baltimore Ruby on Rails consultancy. Register for BohConf.

Ignite RailsConf

Ignite events are lightning talks, where 16 speakers each get 5 minutes to talk about a subject they are passionate about, but with a twist: the speaker's slides are automatically advanced every 15 seconds. Several B'More on Rails members are presenting! Follow @igniterailsconf!

Ride with a Local: taxi service from BWI

A cab ride from BWI airport to downtown Baltimore can cost more than $20. B'more on Rails members will be standing by in the afternoon Sunday June 6 and Monday June 7 to provide personalized rides from BWI to your hotel. We'll be announcing designated pick-up areas at BWI and publishing a real-time taxi locator app. Follow @bmoreonrails for updates.

Baltimore Ambassadors

When you see one of us in a B'More on Rails t-shirt, say hi! Ask us about Baltimore! Whether you need a recommendation for a place to grab a quick beer, or you need to know the best way to get to the other side of town, we're here to help! Look for the insert in your program, and look for us near the exits after the late afternoon sessions!
