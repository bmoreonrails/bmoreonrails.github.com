Ruby Programmers in Baltimore



B'more on Rails is a community of Baltimore folks who use the Ruby programming language and the Ruby on Rails application framework.

We meet monthly and welcome newcomers of all experience levels, whether you've never touched Ruby or have been using it for years.

To learn more about the next meeting and/or register to attend, please visit our meetup.com page for more information.

Where:

  • Meetings at MDLogix: 1216 E. Baltimore St. 21202
  • Hack nights at Beehive Baltimore: 2400 Boston St. #308, 21224
  • Pub nights at Max's Taphouse: 737 S. Broadway, 21231
